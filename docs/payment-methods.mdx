---
title: "Payment Methods & Locations"
description: "Payment methods we currently support"
---

- All credit card brands (American Express, Visa, Mastercard, Discover, etc)
- All debit card brands
- ACH (US only)
- Apple Pay (email us at help@usewinter.com to activate)
- Google Pay

We support customers from most major locations (North America, Europe, LatAm,
Asia, etc). However, there are a few countries we cannot accept payments from,
such as sanctioned countries (Russia, North Korea). In the United States, we
support all 50 states and Washington D.C.
